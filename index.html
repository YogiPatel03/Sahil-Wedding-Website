Frequently Asked Questions

When is the RSVP due?

Please RSVP by June 12th, 2025 so we can finalize all the details and make sure everything is just right!

Can I bring a plus one?

As much as we would love to celebrate with everyone, due to the venue's capacity and space limitations, we kindly ask that you only bring a plus one if it is specifically mentioned on your invitation. It's truly difficult for us to make these decisions, and we appreciate your understanding as we navigate the details of this special day.

Will there be hotel accommodations?

Yes! We have a block of rooms reserved at the Holiday Inn for our guests. More details, including booking links and group rates, are available on the Accommodations page.

What should I wear?

Since this will be a traditional Hindu wedding ceremony, we encourage guests to wear traditional Indian attire if you have it—think vibrant colors, beautiful fabrics, and lots of joy.

That said, we want you to feel comfortable and confident. If you don't have Indian clothes, no worries at all! Wear whatever feels festive to you. Just keep in mind that the ceremony will take place in a Hindu temple, so out of respect for the space:
– Women should wear longer dresses or skirts that extend below the knees and have their shoulders covered
– Men should wear long pants and avoid shorts

Where will the wedding ceremony be taking place?

The ceremony will take place on the BAPS Nashville Mandir campus, in a beautiful outdoor setting surrounded by nature. It's expected to be warm, so we encourage you to dress comfortably and stay hydrated. We'll have ice-cold refreshments and shaded areas available to help keep everyone cool. We can't wait for you to experience the peaceful, joyful energy of this special space with us.

Are children invited?

As much as we'd love to celebrate with everyone, due to space limitations, we're only able to include a certain number of little ones. If your child is invited, they'll be listed on your RSVP count. It's truly difficult for us to make these decisions, and we deeply appreciate your understanding as we plan for an intimate and joyful day.

Will there be accommodations provided for transportation?

To help make your arrivals and departures smooth, the hotel will have shuttles running between the hotel and the airport. More details on the accommodation page.

Please note, there won't be transportation provided from the hotel to the wedding venue on the day of the wedding, so we kindly ask you to plan for your own ride to the venue.

If you have family or friends in Nashville who are willing to host you, we encourage you to consider staying with them to help ensure that hotel rooms remain available for other traveling guests.

If changes or additional accommodations are needed, we will be providing updates and information via the announcement channel. Join us on Telegram to stay updated: https://t.me/+QenzLC99j7RjNmYx

What's the weather like this time of year?

Expect warm and sunny summer vibes in late August. Light, breathable outfits are encouraged—and don't forget your sunglasses and sunscreen! Be sure to stay hydrated throughout the weekend!

What is the schedule of events?

We'll be sharing the full schedule in the coming week via our announcements channel. Join us on Telegram to stay updated: https://t.me/+QenzLC99j7RjNmYx

For now, save the date—the wedding will take place on the morning of Sunday, August 31, 2025.

What should I do if I can't attend?

While we'll miss having you with us on our special day, please know you'll be in our hearts! If you're unable to attend, kindly fill out the RSVP and indicate that you won't be attending so we can plan accordingly.

If you'd still like to be a part of the celebration, we will have a live telecast of the ceremony for you to watch from afar. We will share the link via the telegram announcements channel closer to our wedding date!

What's the best way to reach out to you with questions?

If you have any questions or need anything at all, the best way to reach us is through our wedding email: bhumiandsahil@gmail.com. We're here to help and so excited to hear from you!
