Introducing the Couple: From each other's perspective!

Bhumi

Bhumi is the brightest, warmest presence in every room she walks into. Her laughter is contagious, and her heart is made of pure gold—always finding ways to show up for her family and friends with love and sincerity. She's a proud germaphobe, so if she's not carrying hand sanitizer, chances are she's holding a matcha instead. And if anyone so much as steps outside for five minutes, she'll seriously insist: "We smell like outside—you have to shower." Bhumi constantly challenges those around her to grow, reflect, and become the best versions of themselves—all while embracing them exactly as they are. She'll stay up late to watch basketball games just to show support, even though she's not exactly a fan (despite what she confidently claimed early on). A true travel enthusiast, Bhumi is always ready to explore every corner of the world—but no matter where she lands, she somehow always ends up with sushi and boba in hand.

Sahil

Sahil is calm, clever, and always one step ahead—except when it comes to Bhumi's biggest pet peeve: sitting on the bed in outside clothes. He does it anyway. Daily. It's fine. She's fine. But he more than makes up for it in every other way. Case in point: their very first date at Six Flags. Bhumi casually mentioned she gets dizzy on rides unless she has a fruit punch Gatorade. Most people would pick one up. Sahil rolled up with a trunk full. That pretty much sums it up—thoughtful, over-prepared, and always looking out for the little things that matter. He's the guy who always cooks for Bhumi (without being asked), knows exactly what she needs before she even says it, and will drive hours just to let her sleep in the car. He goes the extra mile in every way, showing love not through grand gestures but through everyday acts of kindness. That's just who he is..

Things to Do in Nashville & Beyond

Nashville isn't just Music City to us—it's home. It's where Bhumi grew up, surrounded by the warmth of family, friends, and memories that shaped who she is today. It's where we've wandered together through the beautiful trails, discovered hidden gems in the lively city, and shared countless special moments that we'll treasure forever. From morning nature walks, shopping days, and unexpected adventures, this city holds so much of our story—each experience made even more meaningful because it's here, in a place that feels like home. More than anything else, it's all about the people, the moments, and the love we share.

As we look forward to celebrating with all of you, here are a few spots we love that we can't wait for you to experience during your trip to Nashville for our weekend.

Shopping & Exploring

Make the most of your time in Music City! Whether you're looking for a little retail therapy, charming neighborhoods, or Instagram-worthy finds, Nashville's got it all:

12 South

A walkable neighborhood filled with local boutiques, coffee shops, and murals (perfect for photos!). While you're there, don't forget to grab a treat from Five Daughters Bakery (ask for their vegan options!)

The Gulch

Stylish, modern, and full of energy—The Gulch is where Nashville goes to be trendy. Think boutique shopping and cool art walls around every corner. It's the perfect spot for a quick photo shoot or a leisurely afternoon of sipping and strolling.

Downtown & Broadway

Live music, honky tonks, and iconic shops line this energetic stretch. Even if you're not into country music, it's worth a visit for the experience. Souvenir shops and boot stores abound—this is your chance to try on a cowboy hat and experience the true southern lifestyle!

Fifth + Broadway

A modern indoor-outdoor shopping and dining destination right in the heart of downtown. You'll find national brands, local eats, and great people-watching.

Opry Mills Mall

For a classic mall experience, Opry Mills has over 200 stores plus entertainment like an aquarium and movie theater. It's just down the road from the Grand Ole Opry, too!

Outdoor Adventures in Nashville

Looking to slow things down and soak in some natural beauty while you're in town? Here are a few of our favorite outdoor spots:

Nashville Zoo at Grassmere

A fun and easy outing—especially for families with kids. Walk through lush grounds and get up close with animals from around the world.

Radnor Lake State Park

Peaceful hiking trails, scenic lake views, and a high chance of spotting deer, turtles, and maybe even an owl or two. It's the perfect nature escape just a short drive from the city.

Cheekwood Estate & Gardens

A gorgeous blend of seasonal blooms, art exhibits, and romantic walking paths. It's the kind of place where you'll want to take your time. Tip: Be sure to grab tickets in advance—this spot is popular!

Centennial Park

A local favorite for laid-back afternoons. You'll find wide-open green space, a charming walking trail, and Nashville's full-scale replica of the Parthenon (yes, really!). Great for a casual stroll or a cozy picnic

Throwback to our first time in Nashville together, exploring the iconic Parthenon and making unforgettable memories!

For the Fun Seekers

Ready for a little adventure between wedding festivities? Nashville has plenty of exciting options to make your visit unforgettable. Whether you're up for some friendly competition or outdoor thrills, here are some awesome activities to try:

The Adventure Park at Nashville

Zip through the trees or conquer the treetop ropes courses. It's a thrilling challenge for all skill levels (and a great way to feel like a kid again!).

Pinewood Social

Bowling, outdoor games, and great vibes—Pinewood Social is perfect for hanging out and having a blast with friends. Whether you're in the mood for a little friendly competition or just relaxing with friends, this spot has something for everyone.

Escape Game Nashville

Think you've got what it takes to solve the mystery? Grab your crew and put your teamwork to the test in one of Nashville's top-rated escape rooms. It's a great way to laugh, puzzle, and bond!

Puttshack

For mini-golf with a high-tech twist, Puttshack delivers a lively, social experience that's perfect for a fun night out. Enjoy drinks, food, and plenty of laughs as you work your way through the interactive mini-golf course.

Topgolf

If you love a little friendly competition, Topgolf offers a modern, fun twist on the classic game. With climate-controlled bays, food, drinks, and games for all skill levels, it's an awesome way to spend time with friends and family while showing off (or just practicing) your swing.

For the Coffee/Boba Lovers

Nashville knows good coffee/boba, and we've done the very important job of taste-testing around town:

Frothy Monkey

A local favorite with a cozy vibe and a great spot to start your day or catch up with friends.

Haraz Coffee House

Coffee with a Turkish twist—and don't miss the pistachio surprise. It's rich, flavorful, and unlike anything else in town.

Crema Coffee Roasters

Minimalist and modern with a focus on quality. Known for smooth espresso, excellent pour-overs, and a killer view of the Nashville skyline from the patio.

Yin Yang Boba (Germantown)

Bhumi swears this is the best boba in Nashville—no debate! The ice is perfectly crunchy, they have a variety of flavors, and the vibes are just right. Treat yourself to her go-to: the Brown Sugar Boba. Your taste buds will thank you!

Bagel (and Biscuit) Stops We Love

Okay, it's not Jersey or NYC—but Nashville holds its own when it comes to bagels. If you're craving a solid schmear or a biscuit with a twist, these are our go-to spots.

When Sahil visited Nashville, Bhumi made it her mission to find the best local bagels that could rival those back home in Jersey. She wanted him to feel at home, one chewy, toasty bite at a time.

We hope you feel the same—and that these spots make you feel right at home too.

Benji's Bagels

True NYC-style bagels to Nashville—with a vibe that's laid-back, local, and seriously delicious. Small-batch, boiled, and baked fresh, Benji's bagels are chewy in the best way and topped to perfection. Bhumi's friends swear by this place, and once you've had a bite, you'll get why. Grab their favorite, Jalapeno cheddar bagel!

Proper Bagel

Trendy, creative, and totally Instagrammable—Proper Bagel is a must for bagel lovers. Located right on Belmont University's campus, this chic spot serves up everything from cream cheese bagels to sandwich bagels, all as delicious as they are photogenic. It's the kind of place where you'll want to try one of everything… and probably take a picture of it all. And yes, it's so yummy!

Jersey Oven

Craving that true Jersey bagel experience? Jersey Oven delivers with East Coast–style bagels that are perfectly chewy, golden, and baked fresh daily. The owner is originally from Jersey, so you know it's the real deal—no shortcuts, no imitations.

Biscuit Love

Not a bagel spot, but worth the detour. Their Southern-style biscuits and famous bronuts are eggless—and ridiculously good.

Maple Street Biscuit Company

Every biscuit here is eggless, and their jams are truly a treat! They also offer eggless cinnabons that are a must-try. The perfect place for a cozy breakfast or brunch.

Nashville's Sweet Spots (Because Life's Too Short Not to Eat Dessert)

Five Daughters Bakery (Vegan options available)

The doughnuts are so good, they'll make you forget every other doughnut you've ever had. Seriously, one bite of the Purist (a favorite of Bhumi, Sahil, and Pooja (Bhumi's sister)—who's basically perfected the art of third-wheeling) and you'll be hooked. Be sure to ask for their vegan options!

Visit: Five Daughters Bakery

Jeni's Splendid Ice Creams (has vegan options)

For the ultimate ice cream experience in Nashville, Jeni's is the place to be! Bhumi and Sahil always make a stop here when Sahil is in town, and it's become a bit of a tradition. Bhumi's go-to flavor is the lavender ice cream (which Sahil always makes fun of her for), while Sahil opts for the indulgent salted peanut butter with chocolate flecks. With creamy, indulgent options that always hit the spot, Jeni's has become a sweet tradition they both cherish.

Visit: Jeni's Splendid Ice Creams

Sunflower Bakehouse (Entirely Vegan)

For sweet-tooth enthusiasts, Sunflower Bakehouse offers a variety of delicious, vegan, and gluten-free baked goods. From cupcakes and cookies to muffins and parfaits, there's something for everyone to enjoy!

Visit: Sunflower Bakehouse

The Baked Bear (Vegan options)

Indulge in a fully customizable dessert experience at The Baked Bear, where you can create your own vegan ice cream sandwich. Choose from their Vegan/Gluten-Free Chocolate Chip Cookie or Vegan/Gluten-Free Fudgy Brownie Cookie, and pair it with Vegan Chocolate Chip ice cream made with oat milk. For an extra treat, ask to have your sandwich warmed up!​

Visit: The Baked Bear

Legendairy Milkshake Bar (Vegan options)

If milkshakes are your thing, prepare to have your mind blown (in the best possible way). Legendairy Milkshake Bar offers indulgent milkshakes with vegan options available on their website! You can enjoy treats like the Vegan Banana Split and the Parent Trap, made with dairy-free ingredients.

Visit: Legendairy Milkshake Bar

Indian Food in Nashville

You can take the Indians out of India, but you can't take the Indian out of Indians (iykyk)! If you're craving authentic Indian flavors, Nashville has some incredible spots to satisfy your spice-loving taste buds. Whether you're in the mood for rich curries, fragrant biryanis, or crispy dosas, these local favorites bring the best of Indian cuisine right to Music City.

Inchins Bamboo Garden (ask for vegetarian and OG-Free Menu)

Located in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere, Inchins Bamboo Garden offers a vibrant dining experience featuring Indo-Chinese cuisine.

Their menu blends traditional Chinese flavors with Indian spices, creating a unique fusion of bold and savory dishes. They also offer an OG-free menu to cater to dietary restrictions.

Visit: www.inchinsonline.com

Honest (Vegetarian Indian Cuisine)

Authentic, Flavorful, and Fully Plant-Based

If you're craving authentic Indian flavors with a plant-based twist, Honest is the spot to visit. Their menu features OG-free, vegetarian Indian dishes made with fresh, flavorful ingredients. From rich curries to aromatic rice dishes and perfectly spiced wraps, every bite offers a true taste of India. It's a must-try for anyone looking for a satisfying, plant-based meal with bold flavors.

Woodlands (Vegetarian Indian)

Authentic Flavors, OG-Free Options

For an incredible vegetarian dining experience, Woodlands Vegetarian Indian offers a wide range of flavorful and OG-free dishes. Their menu features everything from dosas and idlis to punjabi curries. Made with traditional spices and ingredients, every bite delivers an authentic taste of India.

Visit: www.woodlandsvegetarian.co

Vegan-Friendly Sushi in Nashville

While Nashville doesn't have fully vegan sushi spots, these two places offer vegan options. Be sure to inform them you're vegetarian to avoid any cross-contamination.

Blue Sake Sushi Grill

Offers a fantastic vegan menu with fresh rolls and grilled dishes. Be sure to ask which items are OG-free.

Website: www.bluesakesushigrill.com

I Love Sushi

Another great spot for vegan sushi options. Just don't tell them you love sushi too much, or they might start charging you for extra wasabi!

Website: www.ilovesushinashville.com

Pizza & Pasta Perfection

Craving something cheesy, saucy, and absolutely delicious? While these spots aren't fully vegetarian (besides Singa's pizza), they do offer egg-free and OG-free options to satisfy every craving. Whether you're digging into a wood-fired pizza or twirling some fresh pasta, Nashville's pizza and pasta spots have something for everyone.

Penne Pazze (Has egg-free and OG free options)

At Penne Pazze, both the pizza and pasta are eggless, making it a great spot for those with dietary preferences. Their menu offers a variety of delicious dishes, and they also have one OG-free sauce that can be used for both pastas and pizzas, so you can enjoy the best of both worlds. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty pasta or a wood-fired pizza, you'll find something to satisfy your cravings.

Website: pennepazze.net

DeSano Pizza Bakery (OG free options)

Authentic Neapolitan Pizza with a Nashville Twist

For pizza lovers seeking true Italian authenticity, DeSano Pizza Bakery is a must-visit. Their wood-fired oven pizzas are as close to Naples as you can get. The Margherita Classic VPN pizza is a standout, made with certified ingredients and bursting with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil—and, of course, OG-free! It's the perfect spot to indulge without the worry.

Another great choice is the Americana (N.Y. Style), featuring San Marzano tomato sauce, scamorza, and oregano. Just be sure to ask for it without garlic if you're ordering this one!

Website: https://desanopizza.com

Frankies Pizzeria (OG free options)

Delicious Pizzas with a Casual, Walk-In Vibe

For pizza lovers looking for a great meal, Frankies Pizzeria is a must-visit. Their Classic Pizza is OG-free and a fan favorite, topped with Bianco DiNapoli tomatoes, mozzarella, and Sicilian oregano.

For something a little different, try the Classic Sicilian— DiNapoli tomatoes, mozzarella, Pecorino-Romano, and Sicilian oregano.

No reservations needed—just walk in (be sure to enter the Pizzeria, not the building labeled 925 Spuntino) and enjoy!

Visit: Frankies Nashville.

Singas Famous Pizza (All vegetarian- but ask for OG free sauce)

Singas Famous Pizza, a popular New Jersey restaurant now in Nashville, brings a taste of the East Coast to Music City.

With their OG-free menu options, Singas offers a delicious selection of pizzas made with fresh ingredients and signature sauces. Whether you're craving a classic cheese pizza or a unique specialty pie, Singas has something for everyone. With the groom from New Jersey and the bride from Nashville, this local favorite perfectly blends both of their worlds!
